Interpretive Tree TrailsInterpretive Tree Trails A series of wellgraded walking trails traverses Arbor Day Farms orchards croplands grasslands and the oakhickory forest along South Table Creek a tributary of the nearby Missouri River that crosses the property These trails provide opportunities to enjoy trees in both their natural habitat as well as in orchards windbreaks tree plantations and other humanplanted settings Along the trails youll find unobtrusive interpretive panels that provide understanding of the trees their environment and the natural and aboricultural processes that are at work on the farm
