Cold Numbers Thanks to John Evans for the banner What is Cold Numbers Cold Numbers is a fan created set of cards for On the Edge When a number of us learned that the expansion formerly known as Wetworks TEFKA WW was put on hold by Atlas Games we decided to satisfy our need for new cards ourselves The name was spawned from the subject of a letter from John Nephew explaining why TEFKA WW wasnt possible Anyone have a copy of that letter There is a mailing list on which we discuss the set You can subscribe to it by sending a message to macjordomofenrisitsilstuedu with an empty subject line and Subscibe coldnumbers YourRealName in the first line of the message Alternatively you can deal with a real human being at MrsBrinkerfenrisitsilstuedu The digests of the list are stored at the digest storage depot All the cards so far designed can be found at the Cold Numbers Web Page Please feel free to help us in this projectartists especially LINKS Burger Palace The cafe of conspiracy A resource area for On The Edge OIT A nice place to work March The guy who runs this place The Pumpkin Patch Back to my Home Page Please send any comments to March Pumpkin
